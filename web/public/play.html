Use cases

Same engine. Four formulas.

Every card above uses the same replay flow. What changes is the committed W3O1 formula and the interpretation of the outcome surface.

Raffle

Weighted-random single winner from a committed participant list.

Rewards Selection

Weighted-random multi-winner selection. Replay returns the full ordered outcome_ids list.

Trading Competition

Native rank_desc formula with deterministic tie-break by stable input order.

Prediction Market

Native closest_to formula. Replay includes target, scores, and deterministic ordering.
