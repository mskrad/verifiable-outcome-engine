Use cases

Same engine. Four surfaces.

The active blessed set currently covers four reviewer-facing surfaces. The label on each card comes directly from the blessed evidence bundle.

🎟

Raffle

Single-winner draw from a committed participant list. The winner address is fixed only after on-chain resolution and can be replayed from the signature.

🏆

Rewards Selection

Multi-winner selection from one committed weighted list. All selected winners are stored in the resolution and replayed from public RPC data.

📈

Trading Competition

Native rank_desc formula. Top traders by descending score are selected deterministically and replay shows the committed scores.

🎯

Prediction Market

Native closest_to formula. Predictions closest to the committed target win, with deterministic tie-break by input order.
